Lasagna

Description

This spicy lasagna recipe serves 8 and makes the perfect comfort meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Basil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Prepare your tomato sauce. Make a marinara sauce using onion, garlic, and canned tomatoes. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  2. Cook your beef. Brown your ground beef and add salt, pepper, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning. Add the beef to the tomato sauce.
  3. Assemble your lasagna. Spread a cup of sauce on the bottom of your pan, then add a layer of oven-ready lasagna noodles. From there, add a layer of ricotta, a layer of mozzerella, and another layer of sauce. Repeat until you are out of noodles and top with a layer of mozzarella and parmesan.
  4. Bake your lasagna. Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour. For the last 5 minutes, uncover and let the cheese brown lightly. Serve with garlic bread or a side salad.
