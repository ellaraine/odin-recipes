Enchiladas

Description

These enchiladas are made with a homemade sauce, spicy beef, and creamy mozzarella. Try it yourself and you'll never go back to storebought sauce!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Flour tortillas
  • Onion
  • Corn
  • Jalapenos
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Make the sauce. First, make your enchilada sauce using this recipe.
  2. Make the meat filling. Then, brown your ground beef. When there is little to no pink visible, add diced onion and jalapeno and cook for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Season the filling. Season your filling with salt, pepper, chili flakes, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and adobo.
  4. Assemble your enchiladas. Take a tortilla, sprinkle a handful of mozzarella in the center, then top with a spoonful of your meat filling. Place the finished enchiladas side by side in your baking pan.
  5. Bake your enchiladas. Top with more cheese and bake the enchiladas at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Serve with sour cream, hot sauce, and cilantro for garnish.
